This week's #CanvaDesignChallenge: Video with Patterns
With Canva, creating Videos is as easy as making any other design. Not sure where to start? Give this week’s #CanvaDesignChallenge a go. The theme is Patterns so create a short Video (10 seconds or less) using our wide selection of kaleidoscopic visuals and hypnotic prints. Just search for ‘patterns’ in our elements tab, then add your favorites with one click. You can also add text, transitions, or animations if you’re feeling extra creative. Share your Video with us, and you could win a Canva prize pack.
