This is bigger than just science
The All of Us Research Program isn't just any research program.*
All of Us is a research program from the National Institutes of Health (NIH) with a goal of recruiting one million or more people from across the United States to help medical researchers learn more about health. CVS Health Clinical Trial Services™ collaborates with the All of Us Research Program and contributes to the health research effort by raising awareness of All of Us.
All of Us is combining science and diversity, including groups of people who have been left out of health research in the past, which means they will help researchers learn more than ever before about why ALL types of people stay healthy or get sick.
Fortunately, there’s still time for you to join and be a partner in one of the biggest health research programs ever.
